How To Use Email on The Desktop

Get to Your Email Send an Email View and Repy to an Email

Get to Your Email

  1. Begin by viewing the Desktop of your computer.
  2. Go to the upper left hand corner of the desktop and look for the blue and white "Mail" icon.
  3. Double click the icon to launch the mail app.

      4. Mail icons can also be found in two additional locations:

      • There is a white mail icon along your bottom task bar.
      • There is a blue and white mail icon avaiable by clicking the 4 squares in the bottom right of the screen.

Send and Email

  1. Go over to the left side of the screen, and near the top, click the "+ New Mail" button.
  2. Fill in the appropriate fields of the email.
  3. At the top right click the "send" button with the (arrow) icon next to it.

      4. Note: If you wish to discard an email draft, click the (trashcan) icon next to the send button.

View and Reply to an Email

  1. At the Home Screen of your email, go to the white "inbox" column.
  2. Click on, and select, the email you are interested in viewing and/or replying to.
  3. Go to the top of the email, and near the center of the screen, select the (reply) button.
  4. Follow steps described to send an email to send a reply; following from step 2 onward.
  5. Emails may be deleted via clicking the (trashcan).
